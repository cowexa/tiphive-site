Privacy Policy — TipHive

Last updated: July 1, 2026

TipHive ("the app", "we") is a tip-splitting and record-keeping tool. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Information we collect

We do not collect or store your personal information on our servers. TipHive has no account or login.

Data you enter — staff names, hours, roles, tip amounts, role groups, and saved split records — is stored only on your device. It is not transmitted to us and is deleted if you delete the app.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

TipHive is free and supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain data, such as device identifiers, IP address, and ad-interaction information, in accordance with Google's policies.

  • Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

TipHive requests non-personalized ads only and does not track you across apps or websites. You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device settings at any time.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share the information you enter in the app with anyone; it stays on your device. Advertising data is handled by Google AdMob as described above.

Children

TipHive is intended for adults working in the service industry and is not directed to children under 13.

Security

Your entered data is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. Please protect your device to protect your data.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at this page with a new "Last updated" date.
